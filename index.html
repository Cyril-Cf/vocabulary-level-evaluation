Interpretation of Results

Mean Vocabulary Level: This score indicates the average level of vocabulary used in the text. A lower score means the vocabulary is simpler, while a higher score suggests more complex language.

Highest Level: This represents the highest vocabulary rank found in the text. It shows the most advanced word used, giving insight into the complexity of language in the piece.

Grade: The overall grade indicates the estimated proficiency level required to understand the text. It is derived from the percentage of words in different vocabulary levels, helping gauge the text's accessibility.
